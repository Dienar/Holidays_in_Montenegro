Pickup Locations

Discover the various airports where you can conveniently pick up your rental for an unforgettable camping experience in Montenegro. Below, you'll find the key airports across the country.

Podgorica Airport

The main international gateway to Montenegro. Located in the capital city, it is easily accessible and connected to the rest of the country.

Location: Podgorica, Montenegro

Distance from main campsite: 50 km

Tivat Airport

Situated near the coastal town of Tivat, this airport is a convenient option for those arriving via the Adriatic Sea.

Location: Tivat, Montenegro

Distance from main campsite: 85 km

Herceg Novi Airport

Small but efficient, located near the stunning Bay of Kotor, a perfect choice for those staying near the coast.

Location: Herceg Novi, Montenegro

Distance from main campsite: 120 km

Cetinje Airport

A smaller, quieter option, located near the old royal capital. A great choice for those seeking a peaceful retreat.

Location: Cetinje, Montenegro

Distance from main campsite: 150 km
