Important information!

To avoid failure of the battery that powers the entire electrical system of the motorhome, when the on-board voltage drops below 11.4 Volts, the system (the inverter is located between the passenger and driver’s seats) will begin to emit a weak signal and you need to turn off the refrigerator or connect to a 220 volt network or you must have the sun . In cases where there is no sun, you need to connect to an external 220 Volt network at the campsite. To turn on the refrigerator, you must have the sun (the charge comes from a solar battery), or a running car engine (the charge comes from the car generator), or an external power source at the campsite (the charge comes from the built-in 220 Volt transformer). Based on the experience of using this refrigerator, the on-board network can withstand a couple of hours of operation of the refrigerator in the absence of external power supply (no sun or no 220 volts).
